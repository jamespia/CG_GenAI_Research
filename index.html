                    <p class="text-slate-600 mb-4">GenAI's greatest impact is on people. It's not about replacing humans, but augmenting their capabilities. This necessitates a strategic focus on evolving roles, organisational design, and the essential skills required to thrive in a GenAI-augmented workplace.</p>

                    

                    <div class="space-y-4 mb-8">

                                    <span class="text-blue-500 font-bold text-xl">→</span>

                                    <span class="text-slate-700">👩‍🔬 Specialist Leader</span>

                                </div>

                                <p class="text-slate-600 mt-2">GenAI handles broad tasks, allowing managers to deepen their expertise. Only 18% of leaders expect GenAI to reduce middle management roles, while 51% expect it to shift to specialist roles.</p>

                            </div>

                        </details>

                            <p class="mt-2 text-slate-600 text-center">The future isn't man vs. machine, but man "with" machine. GenAI acts as a "co-thinker" and "spartner" partner, handling routine work and freeing humans to focus on tasks that demand creativity, critical thinking, strategic insight, and emotional intelligence. GenAI is expected to facilitate approximately one-third of entry-level tasks, which leaders believe will accelerate career progression. Organisational structures will become flatter, more agile, and inherently collaborative to adapt to this dynamic environment.</p>

                    <h3 class="text-2xl font-semibold mb-4 text-center">From Copilot to Cothinker: A Strategic Shift</h3>

                    <p class="text-slate-600 text-center max-w-2xl mx-auto mb-8">Capgemini's framework highlights two modes of AI interaction. Moving from Copilot to Cothinker is key to unlocking strategic value.</p>

                    <div class="grid md:grid-cols-2 gap-8">

                        <div class="border-4 border-blue-200 p-6 rounded-lg bg-blue-50/50">

                            <h4 class="text-2xl font-bold text-blue-800 mb-3">✈️ Copilot Mode</h4>

                            <p class="text-slate-700 mb-4">AI performs tasks with minimal human intervention. It's about <strong class="font-semibold">speed, automation, and execution.</strong></p>

                            <h5 class="font-semibold mb-2">Typical Use Cases:</h5>

                             <ul class="list-disc list-inside text-slate-600 space-y-1">

                                <li>Drafting emails & meeting minutes</li>

                                <li>Correcting grammar & summarising text</li>

                                <li>Completing or suggesting code snippets</li>

                                <li>Visualising data from a spreadsheet</li>

                            </ul>

                        </div>

                        <div class="border-4 border-purple-200 p-6 rounded-lg bg-purple-50/50">

                            <h4 class="text-2xl font-bold text-purple-800 mb-3">🧠 Cothinker Mode</h4>

                             <p class="text-slate-700 mb-4">Humans and AI collaborate on complex problems, focusing on <strong class="font-semibold">reflection, critical judgment, and co-creation.</strong> This mode nurtures deeper insights and strategic outcomes.</p>

                            <h5 class="font-semibold mb-2">Typical Use Cases:</h5>

                             <ul class="list-disc list-inside text-slate-600 space-y-1 mb-4">

                                <li>Assessing strategic choices & trade-offs in new market entry</li>

                                <li>Framing complex problems like talent retention from multiple perspectives</li>

                                <li>Challenging assumptions in a large-scale transformation plan</li>

                                <li>Cultivating responsible leadership skills through ethical dilemma simulations</li>

                                <li>Co-creating a multi-year product roadmap</li>

                                <li>Evaluating potential risks and mitigation strategies for a global supply chain disruption</li>

                            </ul>

                            <h5 class="font-semibold mb-2">Example Prompts for Cothinker Mode:</h5>

Design a comprehensive leadership development programme that leverages GenAI for personalised coaching simulations. Outline the curriculum, key learning objectives, and methods for assessing the effectiveness of AI-driven leadership training in fostering adaptive behaviours."</p>

                            </details>

                            <details class="bg-purple-100">
